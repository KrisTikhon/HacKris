Cybersecurity as a central pillar.

Cyber attacks have become a major concern for businesses in recent years. As a result, cyber security became an absolute necessity. IT leaders must adopt a pragmatic and systemic approach to adjust cybersecurity optimization priorities continuously. The best basic steps such as using encrypted services, training and raising employee awareness, conducting pentesting tests, creating and updating protocols and software, and validating compliance and governance…. Finally, adopt a Zero Trust approach, a model that assumes potential data breaches and verifies each request as if it came from an uncontrolled network. Thus, each access request is strongly authenticated, authorized within the constraints of the policy, and inspected for anomalies.
